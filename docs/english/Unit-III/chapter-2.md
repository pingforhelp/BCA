# How Dare You *by Greta Thunberg*

# About the Speaker
Greta Thunberg is a swedish ==climate activist==, born in 2003. She began the: 
> [!quote] 
> “Friday’s for Future” 
movement to demand urgent action against climate change. Her bold speech ++“How Dare You”++ at the U.N in 2019 made her a global youth leader. She has inspired millions world wide she has received several international honors, including being nominated for the Nobel Peace prize, & was named “Times Person of the Year” in 2019. Greta represents the power of youth, truth & courage in bringing change to the world.

# Q3. What sacrifices should individual make to protect the environment?
Ans: To protect the environment individuals need to make conscious and responsible sacrifices in their daily lifes. These includes:
- Reduce energy Consumption: using electricity wisely switching to renewable sources & minimizing wastage.
- Limiting use of Plastic: Avoiding single use plastics and opting for eco-friendly alternatives
- Using Public transport (or) Eco-friendly vehicles :- Reducing Carbon emissions from personal vehicles.
- Conserving water and resources : Avoiding waste.

Adopting Sustainable Lifestyle Choices
- Eating less meat
- Recycling
- Planting trees and supporting green initiatives

These sacrifices may require giving up convenience & extra comfort, but they are necessary to ensure a safe healthy & sustainable planet for present & future generations.

-----------

# Q4. How does economic growth conflict with environmental protection? Can both be balanced?
Ans: Economic growth often focuses on industrialisation, mass production & increased consumption which can harm the environment. Activities like deforestation, mining, factory emissions & over use of resources boost the economy but lead to pollution, climate change & loss of biodiversity. In this way short-term profits often come at the cost of long-term environmental health. Balancing Both - It is possible to balance economic growth with environmental protection.

Sustainability
- Using renewable energy instead of fossil fuels
- Promoting green technologies & eco-friendly industries
- Implementing strict environmental laws along side Economic Policies
- Encouraging responsible Consumption and Circular economy practices.

By integrating sustainability into development nations can grow economically without destroying the planet ensuring resources & a healthy environment for future generations!
